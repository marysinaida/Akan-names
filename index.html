AKAN NAMES

Welcome to Akan names application that you going to use it to display your Akan name, Akan names are names given to children in Ghana according to the day of the week they were born("day names"). click here to learn more on Akan names.

Enter your birthday

You have to enter your birthday in the calendar format (mm/dd/yy).Where they mean
  • D=The day
  • M=The month
  • Y=The year
You will input your details by typing or by clicking on the provided calender.

Choose your gender

Secondly , you need to choose your gender either Male or Female field provided since your akan name will be outputed only when you have provided the gender and your valid dates so you need to understand that the gender section is important.

Submit button

The submit button is the part where your iput or your details have been feed to the application and then it produce your or output your Akan name and the day your born in case you forget to choose the gender or the correct date aprompt will appear to inform you to input the correct details.
